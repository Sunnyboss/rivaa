About Us

Rivaa Global, a company specializing in manufacturing engineering services is in the international market since over 7 years now. Every solution we provide comes with a prime focus on improving customer competitiveness through a combination of domain expertise, world class quality processes, business models and strategic advantages.

Our continuously evolving network of partners and subcontractors across the globe, complement and extend our capabilities. From Concept, through design, engineering, testing & manufacture, Rivaa has the best to offer. Product engineering services are based on the global engineering delivery model & capabilities from across the world.

Our process knowledge with highly developed engineering product development skills & software expertise gives us the edge to develop customized solutions with the highest standard of quality & cost efficiency.

Who Is Behind:-
Vidyashankar is the Founder and C.E.O of Rivaa Global. He holds a bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering and post graduate diploma in International Business Operations.

An accomplished executive with 20+ years of distinguished career with proven track record in operations, strategic marketing and business expansion.

Prior to setting up Rivaa Global, he has worked with OEM’s, established engineering services companies and start up environments in driving innovative, cost- effective sales and marketing strategies, programs and initiatives producing dynamic results. He has worked across all levels of Engineering phase: CAD, CAE & PLM (Both in Technical & Business Development) in the Automotive, Aerospace and General Industry.
